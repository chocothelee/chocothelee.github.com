Fullerene

May 2024 - Current

A RCOS Project

Unity, C#



Fullerene aims to create a 3D environment where the user can build and explore their own molecules & experiment with their molecular and atomic geometry in an accurate and detailed simulation.



This project is supervised by Dr. Alexander Ma, and the Rensselaer Center for Open Source (RCOS).
