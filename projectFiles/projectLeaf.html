Project Leaf

December 2021

PT. Cashtree Internship

CSS, JavaScript, EJS, SQL



During my 2 weeks of interning at PT. Cashtree in Jakarta, Indonesia, I worked on the front and back end development of this webpage. Due to the company being a small startup, there was no digital system in place to keep track of company leaves of absences, and making requests were difficult. My goal for this project was to fix this issue by creating a system for the company with an easy-to-use webpage.

Project Leaf was implemented within the company for use after development.
